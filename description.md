# Skyseed

**Terraform the sky, one seed at a time.**

Skyseed is a skyblock where you don't grind your way wider — you *grow* the world outward. Craft a **Skyseed**, throw it into the open air, and seconds later it germinates into a brand-new, procedurally generated floating island. Every throw is a new piece of world: trees, ore, water, whole biomes, blooming out of nothing.

You begin on a small starter island with a single tree. From there, exploration **and** crafting drive your progress — each new island gives you the blocks to craft the next, more ambitious one.

---

## How it works

1. **Craft a Skyseed.** Different recipes make different seeds — dirt + planks for a Forest, stone + cobblestone for a Rocky, snow + ice for a Frozen, and so on.
2. **Throw it.** Hold right-click to wind up, release to launch.
3. **Watch it grow.** After a moment the seed germinates and an island grows in before your eyes.
4. **Harvest, then craft the next seed.** Rinse, repeat, and build your archipelago across the sky.

No two islands are ever the same — every one is rolled fresh within its theme, so you never paste the same blob twice.

---

## Throw it your way

A toggle keybind (default **V**) switches between two throw modes — your choice is remembered:

- **Classic** — a charged toss. A light tap drops the island close; a full charge sends it sailing.
- **Precise** — direct placement. The island appears exactly where you're looking, at a distance set by how long you charge. Perfect for laying out a tidy sky-base.

---

## Eleven islands to discover

Each Skyseed grows its own kind of island, with multiple looks and hidden surprises:

- 🌿 **Forest** — the starter. Grass, flowers, and trees that match wherever you throw it. (A pricier **Large Forest** seed grows a much bigger one.)
- ⛰️ **Rocky** — the miner's island. Every ore is here, and *where you throw it matters*: low throws strike deepslate and diamonds, high throws bring coal and iron — and the highest become snow-capped peaks.
- 🏜️ **Desert** — sand and sandstone, cacti and dead bushes, with clay and buried fossils below. Throw it over a badlands for red dunes.
- 🍄 **Mushroom** — a mycelium isle crowned with giant red and brown mushrooms. Mobs won't spawn here — a safe place to build.
- ❄️ **Frozen** — snow and ice, packed ice and rare blue ice... and hidden powder-snow pockets to watch your step around.
- 🌸 **Meadow** — the flower island. Every dye-flower, towering sunflowers, cherry blossoms, and bee nests tucked among the blooms.
- 🪨 **Badlands** — a mesa in the sky, its cliffs banded in layers of orange, yellow, white, red, and brown terracotta, and rich with gold.
- 🏛️ **Ancient** — a shard of the deep underground hung in the air: deepslate and tuff, amethyst geodes, dripstone teeth, and a rare deep-dark variant bristling with sculk.
- 🌿 **Lush** — a cave garden afloat. Moss, azalea trees, dripleaves, and glow-berry vines trailing off the underside.
- 🌊 **Aquatic** — half land, half floating lake: kelp and lily pads, warm-water coral reefs, mangroves, and clay-rich banks.

---

## A living, biome-aware world

Islands respond to **where** you throw them. A Forest seed over a savanna grows acacia; over a jungle, jungle trees; over an ocean, a little lake island. Throw a Desert over a badlands and it turns red. The sky is a full, varied biome map — your islands take after their surroundings.

And on the mining islands, **altitude matters**: the deeper you place them, the rarer the treasure.

Just about every block you'd dig up in a normal world has a home here — so whatever you're building, there's an island that grows it.

---

## Your field guide

Every world comes with the **Skyfarer's Almanac**, an in-game guidebook. It always shows you how to craft each seed — and once you've made one, it unlocks that island's field notes so you can learn its secrets as you go. (You can also craft the book any time from a book + any Skyseed.)

---

## Getting started

- Chop your starting tree and dig the island for dirt and planks.
- Craft your first **Forest Skyseed** and throw it into the void to grow your second island.
- Open the **Skyfarer's Almanac** for every recipe, and go build a sky.
